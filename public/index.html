Why Xecurra?

The MSME Trade Problem is Real

India's MSMEs lose time, money, and opportunities every day to outdated, fragmented trade processes. Xecurra fixes that.

😰 The Problem
Managing exports & imports is overwhelming
❌ Dozens of disconnected documents across WhatsApp, email, and folders
❌ No visibility on shipment status or customs clearance
❌ Freight rates negotiated blindly, no market benchmarks
❌ Trade finance access is slow, complex, and often denied
😩 The Old Way
Spreadsheets, middlemen & courier visits
📋 Manual paperwork — BOL, LC, commercial invoice typed one by one
📞 Calling freight agents for rates, waiting days for quotes
🏦 Bank visits for every LC, payment certificate, or credit application
😰 One error in customs docs = shipment held, fines, delays
✨ The Xecurra Way
One digital dashboard — instant, compliant, connected
✅ AI-generated trade documents in minutes, not days
✅ Real-time shipment tracking across all carriers & modes
✅ Instant freight quotes from a network of verified forwarders
✅ Digital trade finance — apply, approve, disburse in one flow
