How It Works

Volume-Based Pricing

Freight rates vary by commodity, route, origin, destination, and shipment volume. Here's how each component is priced.

🚢

Sea Freight — FCL

Competitive rates based on container size — 20ft, 40ft, and 40ft HC. Portal-to-port and door-to-door options available across all major trade lanes.

Per container
📦

Sea Freight — LCL

Pay only for the space you use. Per CBM (cubic meter) pricing for smaller cargo volumes that don't require a full container.

Per CBM
✈️

Air Freight

Fast, reliable air cargo services with per-kg pricing. Ideal for high-value, time-sensitive shipments. Door-to-door and airport-to-airport options.

Per KG
🚛

Road Transport

Domestic haulage via FTL and PTL services across India. Per-trip pricing with options for part-truck-load and full-truck-load depending on volume.

Per trip
🏛️

Customs Clearance

Flat fee per shipment regardless of cargo value. Covers all documentation, filing, and clearance at Indian customs — no per-item or ad-valorem charges.

Flat fee per shipment
