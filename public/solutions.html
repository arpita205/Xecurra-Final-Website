For Exporters

Export to the World. Digitally.

From your first export inquiry to final payment collection — Xecurra manages every step so you can focus on growing your business.

1
Buyer Inquiry & Quotation
Receive buyer enquiries, prepare proforma invoices, and share professional export quotations digitally.
2
Trade Documentation
AI-generated commercial invoice, packing list, shipping bill, certificate of origin — error-free, compliant.
3
Freight Booking
Get instant quotes from verified forwarders. Book FCL, LCL, or air cargo — compare rates, book in one click.
4
Shipment Tracking
Real-time container tracking. Share live status with your buyer. ETA alerts and delay notifications.
5
Payment Collection
Forex payment collection and bank realisation — streamlined with Xecurra.

Key Features for Exporters

Shipping Bill Automation
Auto-fill ICEGATE shipping bill from your invoice data. Reduce errors, speed up customs.
Export Document Checklist
Country-specific document checklist. Never miss a required certificate or permit.
Forex Rate Tracking
Live USD/EUR/GBP rates with RBI reference. Lock-in rates for future shipments.
Buyer Creditworthiness
AI-powered buyer credit assessment for new international customers. Reduce payment risk.
Export Analytics
Track your export performance — top markets, product trends, freight cost analytics.
AI Trade Assistant
Ask XecurraAI about HS codes, duties, Incoterms, or any trade query — instant answers.
For Importers

Import Smarter. Clear Faster.

Xecurra simplifies your import process from sourcing to warehouse delivery — with real-time visibility at every step.

1
Supplier Discovery & Sourcing
Find verified international suppliers. Compare prices, MOQs, and lead times in one dashboard.
2
Import Documentation
Bill of lading, import licence, custom duty calculation — AI-assisted and error-checked.
3
Freight & Insurance
Book import freight, arrange cargo insurance, and coordinate with your CHA — all from Xecurra.
4
Customs Clearance
Real-time customs clearance status. Duty payment tracking, examination order alerts.
5
Warehousing & Delivery
Coordinate CFS release, inland transport, and final delivery to your warehouse or buyer.

Key Features for Importers

Duty Calculator
Enter HS code + origin country — get instant BCD, IGST, and total landed cost estimate.
Container Tracking
Live ETA from origin port to destination. Customs exam status and delivery coordination.
Cargo Insurance
Digital marine insurance certificates. One-click coverage for sea, air, and road legs.
Import Documentation
Pre-shipment inspection, customs documentation, and compliance — handled end-to-end by Xecurra.
