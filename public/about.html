Our Story

Built in India.
Built for the World.

We're on a mission to make global trade as simple as a few clicks for India's 63 million MSMEs.

The Problem

Why We Built Xecurra

India is aiming for $2 Trillion in exports by 2030. But the MSMEs driving this growth are stuck in the past — managing complex international trade via WhatsApp, paper documents, and fragmented phone calls.

Every small exporter faces the same hurdles: endless compliance paperwork, opaque freight pricing, and a massive trade finance gap that banks refuse to fill.

Xecurra was born in Faridabad, Haryana to solve this exact problem. We aren't just building software; we are building the digital infrastructure that gives an Indian MSME the same logistics power as a multinational corporation.

We realized that MSMEs weren't struggling to find global buyers — they were struggling with the friction of execution. Xecurra exists to eliminate that friction completely.
